Climate Consequences

The N Vision 74 is powered by a fuel cell that converts the chemical energy of redox reactions, in which oxygen receives electrons from the hydrogen fuel, into electricity. Gas-operated cars emit particles toxic particles into the atmosphere. However, Hyodrgen-powered fuel cells—such as the one in the N Vision 74—emit water and air. The N Vision 74’s storage battery, however, does have some problematic effects on the environment.
